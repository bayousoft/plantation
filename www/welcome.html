One of the most common questions that visitors ask when they arrive in New Orleans is, "So, where are the plantations?" And the answer is, here, in an area just outside of the city known as New Orleans Plantation Country. Each year thousands of people take a short ride to the great river road so that they can explore some of the most beautiful and historic plantation homes in America. Let's take a look.
